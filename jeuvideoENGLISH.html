Story

Our story begin in 2099. At that time , a dictature, calls the Empire, rules the galaxy.
They automatically eliminate the persons who don't agree with their ideas , and that how Elliot family was murdered. Elliot is now an ennemy of the Empire , he is search for big treason and will be sent to death , when he will be found.

Even by the death of his familu , our hero carry on to resist and he is read to get his revenge.
With the help of a friend , he get a travel machine to get back in time , and with this tool he swear to eliminate the Empire. In this fight against the Empire , Elliot is fighting against some robots from the Empire. Nobody knows his creator..
Our hero is searching for him to stop him from creating those robots who paralyse the Resistance against the Empire.
